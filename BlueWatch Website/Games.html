GAMES MODE

Siege


3 Objective Point are needed to win a match. There are:
  • 1. Taking control of a Capture Point (+1)
  • 2. Escorting the Payload into the enemy team's base (+1)
  • 3. Stalling the enemy team's Payload until the time runs out (+1)

    • Payload


    Payload is an Attack / Defend style game mode with two rounds. When round one is complete, both teams swap sides. The Payload only advances when at least one attacker is close to it, but won't move if there are defenders close by.

    Survival


    Survival is a round based death match game mode. To win a round, players must defeat all members of the opposing team before they themselves are defeated. Once slain, players won't respawn until the next round starts.
