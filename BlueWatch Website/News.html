NEW HEROES: Ash, The War Machine


Abilities:

[LMB] Burst Cannon - Fire a shrapnel round every 0.9s that explodes in an AOE dealing 400 damage.


[RMB] Kinetic Burst - your next shrapnel round. When you next fire, the round will instantly explode in a frontal cone, dealing 300 damage and applying a knockback.

[Q] Siege Shield - Deploy a 5000 health siege shield that advances forward. lasts 6 seconds.

[F] Shoulder Bash - Lower your shoulder and hurtle forward. Hitting an enemy will deal 800 damage and apply a strong knockback.

[E] Assert Dominance - Leap forward and Plant your Banner in the ground dealing 350 damage on impact and creating a Damage Immune zone for yourself that lasts 8s.

- Heroes Skin:

2. Body
3. Voice Pack (included with Epic and above)
- New Match Lobby presentation
All team members Heroes models now show on pedestals “We feel that this is a great way for players to get a better sense of their team composition and to show off their skin of choice”
- Toggle Auto purchase items option added

[Xbox One] Re-enabled Stone Keep.

- Fixed an issue where characters would auto-fire abilities after respawning.
- Fixed an issue where players could not navigate to certain collections from the Heroes screen.
- Fixed an issue where certain gameplay options would reset upon logging in.
- Autobuy should no longer reset after being turned off.

GENERAL BUG FIXES
- Fixed issue with new players not getting prompt for Region selection
